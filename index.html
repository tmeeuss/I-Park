App Instructions

  • Step 1: create an account

  • To get to sign up page click sign up button
  • Fill out the form with all of your information.
  • All Fields MUST be filled
  • Step 2: Sign in

  • Log in with the email and password
    you used to sign up

    Select the remember me to remember your login credentials

    If you forgot your password click "Forgot password" to reset your password


  • How to Add parking

    open the navigation drawer
    and select "Add Parking"

    Add your credentials, licence plate must be the same as what you used when you signed up



    Show List of parking recipt

    In navigation drawer select parking list.
    To get more detailed information on the parking recipt click on the cell



    How To Edit Profile

    In the navigation drawer select "Profile"
    Make your desired edits Click submit to update Profile



    Find Parking

    to find parking, in the nav bar select "Map"
    enter in the search bar your desired location



    Logging out

    to Log out click the dots in the top right corner
    click on Logout to return to sign in page



    for more help or questions please contact: help@ipark.com
